The Carlile-Huntington Breakthrough

The Robert side originally had scattered clues: Princeton, Ohio, manufacturing, Huntington, Walloon Lake, and train/summer-house memories. Those clues converged around a Columbus, Ohio family: William Arthur Carlile + Ruth Huntington Carlile.

The strongest bridge came from William Harlow Carlile’s Princeton Alumni Weekly memorial, which says he followed his father William A. ’36, uncle John ’38, and brother Robert ’64 to Princeton. His obituary then identifies him as the youngest child of Mary and William A. Carlile Jr., with siblings Robert and Molly.

Why that mattered

It tied together almost every family-memory clue at once: Columbus Academy, Princeton, Ohio roots, manufacturing, Huntington as a family surname, and Walloon Lake summers.
